Kids Help Phone provides professional counseling and crisis support for youth facing the psychological impacts of technology, such as cyberbullying and digital harassment. By offering a safe, anonymous space to discuss online abuse, they mitigate the long-term mental health risks associated with excessive or toxic social media interactions and digital exclusion.

How to Use: Call the toll-free line for immediate voice support or text "CONNECT" to 686868 to speak with a crisis responder via SMS.

Contact: 1-800-668-6868 | 439 University Ave, Suite 300, Toronto, ON.

Availability: Services are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
