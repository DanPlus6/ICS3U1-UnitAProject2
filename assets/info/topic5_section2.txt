Staples provides a convenient retail solution for the creation of e-waste and ink cartridge disposal. Most Staples locations (excluding small-format stores) accept a wide variety of electronics, including computers, tablets, and printers, as well as ink and toner cartridges. This program prevents hazardous materials from entering the waste stream. To use the resource, simply bring your old tech or cartridges to the customer service desk or the designated recycling station inside the store. It is free for individual consumer use.

Availability: Daily during standard store hours (typically Mon-Fri 9:00 a.m. -- 9:00 p.m.; Sat 9:00 a.m. -- 6:00 p.m.; Sun 10:00 a.m. -- 5:00 p.m.).

Contact: Local store phone numbers vary; visit staples.ca for the nearest location.

Mailing Option: For ink/toner, businesses can often request a pre-paid mailing palette through their online account.
