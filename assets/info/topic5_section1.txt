Elgin Mills Community Environmental Centre helps mitigate the creation of e-waste and the excessive use of paper. Located at 1124 Elgin Mills Road East, Richmond Hill, ON, this depot allows residents to drop off end-of-life electronics (like old monitors and laptops) and shredded paper for responsible recycling. By diverting these materials from landfills, the centre ensures toxic components in electronics are handled safely. Users must provide proof of York Region residency and should clear personal data from devices before arrival.

Availability: Wednesday to Saturday (8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m.) and Sunday (8:30 a.m. – 3:30 p.m.). Closed Mondays and Tuesdays.

Contact: 1-866-665-6752.

How to Use: Drive to the depot, check in with staff, and place items in the designated recycling bins.
